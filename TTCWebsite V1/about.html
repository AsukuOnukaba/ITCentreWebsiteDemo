About The IT Centre

The IT Centre is a recognized leader in innovative solutions that assist individuals, organizations and governments excel in business by providing the much needed resources, skills, and knowledge.

Established in March, 2009, The IT Centre offers an array of businesses, project management consulting services, and training solutions. Since inception, we have dedicated ourselves to meeting client needs and exceeding client’s expectations.

We bring together a multi-disciplined team that is able to undertake every aspect of IT infrastructure design development, outsourcing, installation, training and ongoing support.
