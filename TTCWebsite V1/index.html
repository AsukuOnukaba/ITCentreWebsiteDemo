About Us


The IT Centre is a Training and Management Consulting Company based in Abuja, covering the area of Information Technology to human capacity development. We have a mandate to aid the growth and sustainable development of corporate instutions across Africa through providing innovative and cutting-edge IT solutions and human capital development.


In addition to our bouquet of services, we are an approved computer-based assessment Centre by world renowed test administration company ETS (Educational Test Service) owners of prometric Test Center which are also part of Pearson VUE and Certiport Test center. Our test partners have clients across the globe in the licensure, certification, academic admissions, regulatory testing and government testing. We provide test center facilities to our clients for both examinations and/or recruitment exercise.

IT consulting

We provide expert advice and solutions in the field of Information Technology to help businesses optimize their IT infrastructure and operations.

People consulting

Our services focus on enhancing human capital through recruitment, training, performance management, and organizational development strategies.

Strategy & Process consulting

We offer strategic planning and advisory services to assist organizations in defining goals, formulating strategies, and achieving competitive advantages in their respective industries.

Our team helps clients effectively manage projects and build organizational capacity through training, mentoring, and skill development programs.

In-door/Out-door Training

We conduct customized training sessions either in indoor or outdoor settings, catering to the specific needs and preferences of our clients.

Computer Base Testing (CBT) Facilities

We provide state-of-the-art facilities for conducting computer-based tests, assessments, and examinations, ensuring reliability, security, and efficiency in the evaluation process.
